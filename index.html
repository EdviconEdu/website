We're affiliated with industry leaders to enhance the quality of services we provide to our members. Our members get the opportunity to be updated with the rapidly changing technology and be the first to learn new technologies while enhancing their educational qualifications.

Microsoft 365 for Education

Empower educators to unlock creativity, promote teamwork, and provide a simple and safe experience in a single, affordable solution built for education.

Benzyme Ventures

Benzyme Ventures is an ambitious organization dedicated to Biotech/ bioinformatics Research and Development. Its main goal is to build up the biotech startup ecosystem in Sri Lanka and fill the knowledge gap between Industry and Academics.

G Suite for Education

With G Suite for Education, educators can create opportunities for learning, streamline administrative tasks, and challenge their students to think critically—all without disrupting current workflows. G Suite for Education tools are powerful on their own, but work even better together.

Loom Pro for Education

Loom is a video recording tool that helps you get your message across through instantly shareable videos. With Loom, you can record your camera, microphone, and desktop simultaneously. Your video is then instantly available to share through Loom's patented technology.

Figma for Education

Figma is a cloud-based design tool that is similar to Sketch in functionality and features, but with big differences that make Figma better for team collaboration. For those skeptical of such claims, we’ll explain how Figma simplifies the design process and is more effective than other programs at helping designers and teams work together efficiently.

SoftMaker Office for Education

Let your documents speak for themselves: Thanks to its intuitive way of use, its huge feature set and outstanding performance, you'll create first-class documents, spreadsheets and presentations with SoftMaker Office.

Canva for Education

Canva is a graphic design platform that allows users to create social media graphics, presentations, posters and other visual content. It is available on web and mobile, and integrates millions of images, fonts, templates and illustrations.


Sustainable Education Foundation

SEF is a volunteer driven not for profit organisation with the short term goal of making the Sri Lankan education system the most effective and most dynamic education system in the world with the help of a specialist global network of local and expat Sri Lakans.

Jigsaw, a Google Company

Jigsaw is a unit within Google that forecasts and confronts emerging threats, creating future-defining research and technology to keep our world safer.

Grammarly, Inc.

Grammarly is an American-based technology company that offers a digital writing assistance tool based on artificial intelligence and natural language processing.

OnTheHub by Kivuto Solutions

OnTheHub is dedicated to removing the barriers to education by providing students and faculty with free and discounted software from the world’s leading publishers.

JetBrains

JetBrains s.r.o. is a Czech software development company whose tools are targeted towards software developers and project managers. They provides free access to all JetBrains IDEs and team tools for educational institutes.
